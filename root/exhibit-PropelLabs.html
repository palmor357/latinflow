About us

Propel Labs’ new Bigfoot Spectral Cell Sorter with SQSoftware provides the power, safety, performance, and flexibility for your lab today and into the future. The Bigfoot can be configured with up to nine lasers and 60 detectors, providing the versatility for both standard fluorescence detection and spectral unmixing. In addition, with a custom designed, integrated Class II biocontainment cabinet, the Bigfoot provides safety and protection without compromising high parameter sorter performance.
