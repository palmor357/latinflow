About us

Depend on Bio-Rad for tools, technologies and expertise to enable cellular, genomic and proteomic analysis. Bio-Rad provides instrumentation and reagents for droplet digital PCR, conventional and real-time PCR, flow cytometry, xMAP technology, Western blotting, chromatography, cell counting, cell imaging and antibodies.
