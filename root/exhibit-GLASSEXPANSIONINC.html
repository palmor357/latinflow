About us

Glass Expansion has four decades of experience manufacturing sample introduction components for ICP-MS and has earned a world-wide reputation for quality and reliability. Continuing this tradition, we are pleased to announce the release of our Fluidigm Helios TOF-ICP-MS Product line. Glass Expansion is also proud to be the first and only interface cone manufacturer with the required technology and equipment to produce the full range of interface cones entirely in-house.
