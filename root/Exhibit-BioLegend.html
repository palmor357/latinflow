About us

BioLegend enables life science discovery from research to cure by providing world-class quality reagents and tools at an outstanding value, with highly acclaimed service and support. BioLegend offers the broadest selection of fluorophore conjugates for multi-color flow cytometry. Our scientists collaboratively develop cutting-edge research and diagnostic products, establishing BioLegend as a global leader in immunology, cancer, neuroscience, proteogenomics, and stem cell research.
