About us

Sysmex offers a broad range of flow cytometry solutions including flow cytometers, monoclonal antibodies, reagents and disposables (including CellTrics cell strainers) for Research Use Only in academic, industrial and clinical research. See what’s new in flow cytometry from Sysmex, the global leader in hematology. For more information, visit www.sysmex.com/flowcytometry.
