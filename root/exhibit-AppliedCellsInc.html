About us

Applied Cells creates revolutionary cell separation and enrichment products that can help identify tumor cells at their rarest stages, before symptoms strike, enabling early diagnosis and intervention. Our proprietary modular MARS System provides an elegant solution for separation, purification and enrichment of target rare cells, including tumor and immune cells, from whole blood and tissues, with high accuracy and high recovery. We offer full automation and programmable process flow.
