About us

With great pleasure, we welcome you to the 35th Congress of the International Society for the Advancement of Cytometry.

At Miltenyi Biotec, we strive to supply all the instrumentation, reagents, and service to support the expanding needs of you as a researcher. Our innovative tools support research at every level, from basic research to translational research to clinical application.

This year we especially focus on automation and standardization in cell analysis. Discover our solutions for flow cytometry and imaging here at our virtual booth. We have pre-recorded two tutorials that highlight the advantages of recombinant antibodies in flow cytometry and gentle cell sorting in next-generation sequencing, that you can find within the program of CYTO Virtual 2020.

 

Do you have a question? Simply use the chat function, we look forward meeting you virtually. Join the flow revolution!

 

Yours,

 The Miltenyi Biotec Cell Analysis Team
